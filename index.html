AMC

AMC Clinical Trails Patient aka AMC CTP is a mobile application which is paired to various A&D BT(Bluetooth) Enabled devices like Automatic Blood Pressure Monitor,Personal Health Scale,Pedometer,spirometer, glucose etc.Once the patient checks BP or Scale etc the paired device which is called source sends the data as byte stream to registered mobile device aka Access Point (AP).The Received byte stream is converted/parsed to proper format and saved in the local database,if internet is available the data is sent to registered web application. Registration happens through a captcha generated on the web.
Roles & Responsibilities: Team Member,Responsible for pairing the mobile device with various A&D Medical devices and getting the byte stream from source and parsing the data and saving in local database or sending to server.

  • Client: AMC Health
  • Date: October 2015 - Present
  • Service: Mobile Development
